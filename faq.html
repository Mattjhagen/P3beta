Frequently Asked Questions

What is P3 Lending?

P3 Lending is a decentralized, peer-to-peer lending platform using blockchain and Bitcoin to enable global, transparent, and trustless lending without intermediaries.

How does crypto lending work?

Crypto lending lets users borrow or lend funds using cryptocurrency as collateral. Smart contracts automate the process, and reputation is built on on-chain activity, not traditional credit scores[2][4][5].

What are the main types of crypto lending?

CeFi (Centralized Finance): Platforms like BlockFi and Coinbase, usually requiring KYC.
DeFi (Decentralized Finance): Platforms like Aave and Compound, no central authority, using smart contracts[2][4].

What are the benefits of using P3 Lending?

  • Lower fees and interest rates compared to banks
  • Global access and 24/7 availability
  • Transparent, immutable transaction records
  • Earn passive income as a lender

What are the risks?

  • Market volatility can affect collateral value
  • Smart contract vulnerabilities (mitigated by audits)
  • Regulatory changes in different regions

Do I need KYC to use P3 Lending?

For small loans, no KYC is required. For larger transactions and to comply with regulations, some verification may be needed, similar to industry best practices[2][4][5].

What assets can I use as collateral?

Bitcoin is the primary asset, but future updates will allow stablecoins and select altcoins as collateral.

How is my reputation built?

Reputation is based on your on-chain lending and borrowing history, not your traditional credit score. Timely repayments and positive interactions improve your score.

How do I get started?

  1. Create a crypto wallet
  2. Connect to P3 Lending
  3. Start lending or borrowing with Bitcoin
